Welcome to Funtasia

Funtasia Water Park is a Water Theme Park located in Varanasi. It is India's best resort & waterpark which is spread across an area of 1.4 acres of land situated at Chandmari Lodhan, Varanasi- 221003.

It's a unique blend of Indian Hospitality & entertainment which offers a sense of privacy & peace, the ideal escape in calm countryside from the pressures of a busy schedule. Funtasia Water Park is coolest Water Park. The Facility boasts of 4 pools, a variety of slides, flumes and other interesting features.

There is something to do for all ages, the mild and strong of heart. Rediscover the child in you as you hurtle down the slides.
