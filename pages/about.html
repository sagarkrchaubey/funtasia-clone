About Us

Discover the ultimate water adventure at Varanasi's premier water theme park

Welcome to Funtasia Water Park

Funtasia Water Park is a Water Theme Park located in Varanasi. It is India's best resort & waterpark which is spread across an area of 1.4 acres of land situated at Chandmari Lodhan, Varanasi- 221003.

It's a unique blend of Indian Hospitality & entertainment which offers a sense of privacy & peace, the ideal escape in calm countryside from the pressures of a busy schedule.

Funtasia Water Park is coolest Water Park. The Facility boasts of 4 pools, a variety of slides, flumes and other interesting features. There is something to do for all ages, the mild and strong of heart.

Our Exciting Features

There is something to do for all ages, from the toddler to the old, from the mild to the strong of heart!

Thrilling Water Slides

Experience the adrenaline rush on our variety of thrilling water slides. From steep drops to twisting tunnels, our slides cater to all excitement levels.

Four Swimming Pools

Enjoy our four different swimming pools designed for various age groups and swimming abilities. From kids' pools to deeper pools for adults.

Fun Flumes

Our specially designed flumes offer a more gentle water experience, perfect for those who prefer a relaxed ride through flowing water.

Kids Play Area

A dedicated area for the little ones with safe, shallow water features, mini slides, and interactive water play equipment.

Event Spaces

Perfect for birthdays, family gatherings, corporate outings, and school trips. Contact us for group bookings and packages.

Food & Refreshments

Enjoy a variety of tasty food options and refreshing beverages at our food court to refuel after all the water adventures.

Why Choose Funtasia?

Experience the perfect blend of fun, safety, and comfort at Varanasi's premier water park destination

Safety First

Your safety is our priority. All our rides and pools are continuously monitored by trained lifeguards. We maintain strict water quality standards and regularly inspect all equipment.

Fun For Everyone

From the toddler to the old, from the rural to the urban, Funtasia has attractions for all age groups and preferences. No one feels left out here!

Cleanliness & Hygiene

We maintain the highest standards of cleanliness throughout the park. Our pools are treated regularly, and all areas are sanitized frequently.

Convenient Location

Located at Chandmari Lodhan, Ring Road Phase-I, near Kanudih Puliya, Varanasi, we're easily accessible while offering a peaceful environment away from the city's hustle.

Ready for an Unforgettable Water Adventure?

Visit Funtasia Water Park for a day full of thrills, fun, and memories. We're open daily from 11:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
